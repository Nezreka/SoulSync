**SoulSync 2.8.4** is out 🎉

🕸️ **Artist Web** — an interactive WebGL map of your whole library. pan/zoom thousands of artists laid out by how they relate, in three lenses: **Taste Map** (by genre), **Communities**, and the **Discovery Web** — where your owned artists anchor a map that grows out to similar artists you *don't* own. click to expand it, add anything to your watchlist, and play artist radio or 30s previews right from the graph.

🎚️ **Quality Profiles** (#974, thanks @nick2000713) — the single global quality setting becomes named, editable profiles. targets, upgrade behavior, AcoustID strictness, downsampling + lossy-copy all resolve per profile, Auto-Import can run its own, and there's an "upgrade until target" cutoff.

🧭 **The Adventurousness dial, for real** — it was mostly cosmetic before (a cache bug). now it actually drives *which* artists surface: deeper the further you push it, genre-diverse, freshly rotated each visit, with "off your usual path" chips at the exploratory end.
